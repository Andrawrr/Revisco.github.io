Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Revisco's cybersecurity services.

Common Questions

How can this benefit our company?

Revisco enhances your company's security posture by identifying vulnerabilities before they are exploited. This reduces the risk of data breaches, protects your reputation, and ensures business continuity, ultimately saving you money on potential downtime and recovery costs.

What specific threats do you protect against?

We protect against a wide range of threats including ransomware, phishing attacks, malware, insider threats, and DDoS attacks. Our systems monitor network traffic and endpoints 24/7 to detect anomalies.

How quickly can you implement security measures?

Implementation time varies based on the size of your organization and the selected plan. Typically, our Starter and Professional plans can be deployed within 1-2 weeks, while Enterprise solutions may require a custom timeline for full integration.

Do you offer 24/7 monitoring and support?

Yes, all our plans include 24/7 threat monitoring. The Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support with dedicated account managers to ensure rapid response to any security incidents.

What happens if we get breached?

In the event of a breach, our incident response team is activated immediately. We help contain the threat, analyze the damage, and assist with recovery and reporting to ensure compliance with legal requirements.

Is your service compliant with industry standards?

Yes, Revisco's solutions are designed to help you meet compliance standards such as GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, and ISO 27001. We provide regular audits and reporting to assist with your compliance needs.
